Building a Cohesive Culture

Our strategic direction moves towards the "Collaborate" quadrant. This shift emphasizes long-term development and building a high-trust environment.


  • Focus: Teamwork, shared ownership, and consensus.
  • Goal: Increase interaction and rapport.
  • Style: People-oriented organization.

Cultural Pillars

Extreme Ownership

When everyone owns outcomes—not just tasks—creativity and initiative naturally increase. We stop blaming and start solving.

Speed Over Perfection

Innovation dies when people wait for perfect answers. We adopt a "ship fast, learn fast" mindset to stay ahead.

Consensus & Consensus

While we move fast, we move together. We prioritize knowing the strengths and weaknesses of every teammate to harness collective power.

Leading Change: The 8-Step Roadmap

Adopting Kotter’s methodology to drive our transformation:

  1. Create a Sense of Urgency Identify potential crises or major opportunities to spark motivation.
  2. Build a Guiding Coalition Form a strong leadership team with enough power to lead the change.
  3. Develop a Vision and Strategy Create a clear vision to direct the change effort and strategies to achieve it.
  4. Communicate the Vision Share the vision transparently across the organization to gain alignment.
  5. Empower Broad-Based Action Remove obstacles and enable employees to participate effectively in the change process.
  6. Generate Short-Term Wins Achieve and celebrate small achievements to build momentum.
  7. Consolidate Gains & Produce More Change Use early successes as a foundation to drive deeper, broader change initiatives.
  8. Anchor New Approaches in Culture Sustain the change by incorporating it into organizational values and culture.
